# WEDE5020
WEB DEVELOPMENT PROJECT
## project Title
Doughzaar
## website information
1.	ORGANISATIONAL OVERVIEW
•	Name: Doughzaar
•	Brief history: Doughzaar is a small, artisanal donut business that is focused on handcrafted, gourmet donuts made with high quality ingredients. The business started as a home based operation and has grown a loyal local following through word of mouth and weekend markets
•	Mission: Its mission is to create joy through handcrafted donuts that combine classic comfort with creative, high quality flavours.
•	Vision: To become the most loved local donut brand, known for exceptional taste and memorable customer experiences.
•	Target Audience: Local residents, young professionals, families and event planners who see premium, indulgent treats for personal enjoyment, gifting or special occasions.
2.	WEBSITE GOALS AND OBJECTIVES
•	Goals: 
o	Establish an online presence to showcase products and brand story.
o	Enable online ordering for pickup and local delivery to increase sales.
o	Generate leads for catering and event orders
•	KPIs to measure success:
o	Traffic: 500 unique monthly visitors within three months of launch.
o	Conversions: 20 online orders per week (pickup/delivery).
o	5 catering or event inquiries per month via the contact form
o	Engagement: Average session duration of 2 minutes.
3.	CURRENT WEBSITE ANALYSIS
•	As Doughzaar currently has no website this project will create a new digital storefront from the ground up allowing for a modern, mobile first approach without the constraints of legacy design
4.	PROPOSED WEBSITE FEARURES AND FUNCTIONALITY
•	ESSENTIAL PAGES:
o	Homepage: High quality hero image of donuts, clear call to action (order now) and a preview of the weekly flavour line up.
o	Products page: Categorised listing of classic, gourmet and seasonal donuts with description, pricing and dietary information.
o	About us page: The story behind Doughzaar, the baking philosophy and the commitment to quality ingredients.
o	Catering and events page: Information on custom orders, pricing tiers and a lead generation form.
o	Contact and location page: Store/pickup address, operating hours, map integration and a contact form
•	Functionality
o	Online ordering system with secure payment gateway integration
o	Inventory management to disable sold out flavours automatically
o	Social media feed integration (Instagram) to showcase new creations
5.	DESIGN AND USER EXPERIENCE
•	Colour scheme: A warm and appetising palette: deep navy blue for sophistication, soft cream for a bakery feel and a vibrant coral pink as an accent colour for call to action buttons (Hynes, 2023)
•	Topography: Primary font: clean and modern headings).
Secondary font: serif for body text to convey artisanal quality
•	Layout and design: A clean< mobile responsive grid layout the prioritises high quality photography. The design will use ample white space to make the product image stand out (Jones and Williams, 2024)
•	User experience considerations
o	Navigation: simple, sticky navigation bar with clear links to menu, About and order.
o	Mobile first approach: As most local customers will browse on smartphones the ordering process will be optimised for touch and speed (Brown, 2025)
o	Visual hierarchy: The weekly special donut will be prominently featured to drive impulse purchases.
6.	TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS
•	Hosting: shared hosting plan to accommodate moderate traffic with room for growth
•	Domain name: doughzaar.co.za
•	Content management system: WordPress with WooCommerce for flexible e commerce capabilities
•	Programming languages: HTML, CSS, JAVASCRIPT
7.	TIMELINE AND MILESTONES
•	It will take up to 8 weeks to officially launch the website
Week 1 = discovery and content gathering
Week 2 = design ( wireframes + mockups )
Week 3 – 4 = frontend development
Week 5 = backend integration
Week 6 = testing 
Week 7 = training and launch
Week 8 = post launch support
