Our Story

From a home kitchen to a beloved local donut destination

How Doughzaar began

Doughzaar started in 2020 as a weekend passion project in a tiny home kitchen. Founder and head baker Thabo Molefe grew up watching his grandmother make traditional magwinya (vetkoek) and wanted to reimagine those comforting flavors with a gourmet twist.

What began as a small stall at the Observatory Neighborhood Market quickly gained a loyal following. Customers lined up for the signature brioche donuts and unexpected flavor combinations. Within a year, Doughzaar moved into a dedicated bakery space, but the heart of the operation remains the same: small batches, real ingredients, and donuts made with joy.

Today, Doughzaar is a Cape Town favorite, known for handcrafted donuts that blend classic comfort with creative, high‑quality flavors. Every donut is still mixed, shaped, and glazed by hand.
